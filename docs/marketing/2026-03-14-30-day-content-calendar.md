## Ground Rules

- LinkedIn is the primary channel. Start here before anything else.
- X/Twitter mirrors or adapts LinkedIn content. Do not create separate X content until LinkedIn rhythm is established.
- Reddit: one post only, at a milestone. Do not post before 100 unlocks.
- One post per day maximum. Two to three per week is the target.
- Every post connects back to the core positioning: one answer, fast, no comparison table.
- Do not post without something real to say. Skip a day rather than post filler.

## Content Health Checks

Run these at the end of each week before planning the next:

- Did any post generate meaningful comments? Follow up in the thread.
- What was the highest-engagement post? Write more like it.
- What was the lowest-engagement post? Do not repeat the format.
- Is the follow-the-build capture growing? If not, test the backup copy variant.
- Is the unlock rate above 10%? If not, flag for copy experiment.

---

## What to Skip Entirely in Month 1

- Listicles ("5 reasons to use TrustMeBroAI")
- Motivational content ("AI is changing everything")
- Reposts or quote-tweets without original thought
- Product screenshots without context or story
- Follower milestone posts
